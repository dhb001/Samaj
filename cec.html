SCLP SAMAJ CUTCHI ECONOMIC COUNCIL (CEC), NAIROBI

ABOUT CUTCHI ECONOMIC COUNCIL

The Cutchi Economic Council (CEC) is dedicated to promoting economic growth and business development within our community. We work to create opportunities for networking, mentorship, and business support for our members.

SERVICES

  • Business networking events
  • Entrepreneurship workshops
  • Business mentorship programs
  • Economic development initiatives
  • Community business support
