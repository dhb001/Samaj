SCLP SAMAJ BUILDING & FLAT LIASING, NAIROBI

ABOUT BUILDING & FLAT LIASING

The Building & Flat Liasing committee is responsible for managing and maintaining the Samaj's properties, including residential and commercial spaces. Our goal is to ensure efficient property management and provide quality housing solutions for our community members.

SERVICES

  • Property management and maintenance
  • Tenant relations and support
  • Facility maintenance and repairs
  • Property development and renovation
  • Community housing assistance
