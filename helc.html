ABOUT HIGHER EDUCATION LOANS

The Higher Education Loans Committee (HELC) is committed to supporting the educational aspirations of our community members. We provide financial assistance through loans to help students pursue higher education and achieve their academic goals.

SERVICES

  • Educational loan programs
  • Student financial counseling
  • Academic support services
  • Scholarship information
  • Educational guidance
