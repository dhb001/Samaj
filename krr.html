SCLP SAMAJ KARIBU RAPID RESPONSE, NAIROBI

ABOUT KRR

The Karibu Rapid Response (KRR) committee was established to provide immediate assistance and support to community members during emergencies and crisis situations. Our dedicated team works round the clock to ensure that help reaches those in need as quickly as possible.

SERVICES

  • Emergency response and assistance
  • Disaster relief coordination
  • Community support during crises
  • 24/7 helpline for emergencies
  • Coordination with emergency services
