SCLP SAMAJ MAHILA MANDAL (LADIES WING), NAIROBI

ABOUT MAHILA MANDAL

The Mahila Mandal (Ladies Wing) is dedicated to empowering women in our community through various social, cultural, and charitable activities. Our committee organizes events and programs that promote women's welfare, education, and cultural preservation.

ACTIVITIES

  • Cultural programs and celebrations
  • Women's health awareness camps
  • Skill development workshops
  • Charitable initiatives
  • Community service projects
