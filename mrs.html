SCLP SAMAJ MEDICAL RELIEF SCHEME (MRS), NAIROBI

ABOUT MRS

The Medical Relief Scheme (MRS) was established in 1992 with the primary objective of providing financial assistance to members of the Samaj for medical treatment. The scheme is managed by a committee of dedicated volunteers who work tirelessly to ensure that members receive the support they need during times of medical emergencies.

SERVICES OFFERED

  • Financial assistance for medical treatment
  • Support for hospitalization expenses
  • Assistance with medical equipment and supplies
  • Guidance on healthcare facilities and specialists
  • Regular health check-up camps
