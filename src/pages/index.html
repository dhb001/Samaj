About Our JOURNEY

A legacy of community service and unity since 1946

9th March 1946

The first meeting of Samaj was held at the premises of Manji Arjan and Company where Mr. Karsanbhai Devraj Vekaria of gaam Baladia was appointed as the first chairman. The main objective of this meeting was to build a community (Samaj) building in Mombasa, Kenya and to raise funds for the same.

6th April 1946

First meeting was convened to deliberate on the rules and regulations of the Samaj.

3rd January 1948

The foundation stone laying ceremony of Mombasa Samaj was conducted.

15th August 1949

Opening ceremony for Mombasa Samaj.

25 August 1951

Proposal to construct a building for Nairobi Samaj was put forward by Mr. Ramjibhai Kunverji of gaam Baladia.

24 November 1951

Approval for construction of Nairobi Samaj Building was given.
