SCLP SAMAJ YOUTH LEAGUE, NAIROBI

ABOUT YOUTH LEAGUE

The Youth League is dedicated to engaging and empowering young members of our community. We organize sports events, cultural activities, and social programs to foster unity, leadership, and personal development among our youth.

ACTIVITIES

  • Sports tournaments and competitions
  • Cultural and social events
  • Leadership development programs
  • Community service initiatives
  • Youth networking events
