SCLP SAMAJ YOUTH LEAGUE, NAIROBI

MISSION AND VISION

  • To enhance educational, social moral, physical, cultural and financial advancement of the members and our community.
  • To undertake programmes for the relief of illiteracy, poverty and/or distress of the needy.
  • The league shall endevour to participate and assist the samaj in organizing its activities.
  • To promote activities for the well being of our youth with a view to fostering unity and brotherhood amongst out society.

HISTORY

Formed in the year 1992, the initiative to start the youth league and on 03rd September 1992, the managing committee gave permission to form this committee with a change in the constitution.

Officially formed on 22nd November 1992, they cultivated friendships and used sports, culture and education to build bridges within the community.

The energetic team published our first ever official newsletter in November 1992; it became the best medium to pass on information to individuals of the community. Saastha, as it was named in June 2009, continues to keep the community updated on all activities of samaj along with enlightening us on different theoretical aspects of life.
